    i'm not dead i swear

    25/02/2026

    I hope everyone's having a good 2026 so far!

    Yes, yes, I did disappear from the face of the indie web for approximately five months now (last updated was 1st september, oops). I guess it's sort of reasonable when I started this website as a summer bored project so inevitably when university starts I kind of got sucked back into the 'serious world' again.

    My experience in London has been fairly positive despite all that I've heard about it. For one it's a pleasant change of pace from the really nice but also really small country I came from. And also, there's just so much fun things to do here. It's such an amazing feeling to live next to all the famous stuff that I can just casually visit during my study breaks.

    As for university, the feelings' been kind of mixed. For one, though the school work has been really REALLY challenging and I've been bogged down in endless reading for the past three months, I actually don't feel all that miserable (for the majority of the time at least, lol)

    At the same time, the work is taking a lot of time out of me. And along with balancing being with friends and just taking care of myself (which i now have to do away from home lol), I really don't have lots of time to do anything else. That's probably the main reason why i neglected this website so much oops. And also now I have to worry about internships and stuff (i'm a first year wtf) which is a whole nother can of worms

    Speaking of taking care of myself, I do find myself somehow getting infinitely better at that since coming to uni. For some reason the moment I get home I just immediately become a slob again. My motivation needs to be studied lol.

    Anyways, recently I've been obsessed with the song 'Drag Path' by twentyonepilots (a lot of people are mad cuz they released a different version of this song than the original paid version but tbh im not all that bothered) and it's got me thinking, what's my drag path?

    I'm not sure how long neocities will stay up for, or how long they'll keep my website up. But if it stays forever, maybe it'll be the most complete drag path i'll have.
