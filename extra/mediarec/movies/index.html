Movie and TV show reccomendations

Movies

The Grand Budapest Hotel

My first and favorite work of Wes Anderson. Aside from the cinamatography and aesthetic, the plot is full of humor and sarcasm and sometimes even subtle symbolism. A fun yet meaningful watch for sure.

Warnings: nudity, sexual themes, dark humor.

The Peanuts Movie

I don't care what anyone says this will forever be my comfort film. Not only does it captures the original comic vibes with 3D animation, its such a cute and comforting movie.

Your Name

I'm not an anime fan but this movie absolutely has my heart oml. The visuals, the characters dynamic, the plot (twist), and don't even get me started on the music. Comfort movie #2.

Legally Blonde

Super fun and inspirational movie about a 'dumb blonde' succeeding in Harvard Law, and still looking extremely pretty while doing so too. Elle Woods you will forever be my idol.

Clueless

A classic teen romantic comedy. Yeah it's kind of predictable but still a really fun watch when you're in the mood for some feel-good romance. Also the love inteest is a tad bit questionable but ngl they look cute together.

Warnings: mention of sex, drugs, and alcohol

Enola Holmes

I should probably rewatch this film since I was too young when I first watched it to understand the mystery lol. But I love the historical settings and costumes, and also the historical implications of women sufferage and other reforms at the time. Also louis partridge is fine af.

TV Shows

Gossip Girl

I've finished the first season and I honestly love it??? its so messy but so good that it makes me feel like my life is boring (is that a bad thing lol)

Warnings: sexual themes, drugs, alcohol

Extraordinary Attorney Woo

This show tells the story of a girl on the autism spectrum tackling her life as an attorney. Eveery episodes is about a new case and, aside from the law and mystery stuff, I also love young woo's character growth.

Witch at Court

Another legal kdrama but much more dark in nature compared to attorney woo. It focuses around SA related cases and can sometimes gets real dark, but the series still has some lighthearted moments. also i-deum is such a girl boss.

Warnings: HEAVY on the sexual assault theme; suicide attempt, some blood, fucked up sex trafficking scheme.

Wednesday

I love the portrayal of Wednesday Adams and the school as a whole in this show. The plot is also sooo fun but also perfectly mixed with mysteries. also the outfits unironically slap.

Warnings: blood, very very light gore
